Spring of your Junior Year!


It’s the spring of your junior year! Aren’t you excited? Maybe you are or you may even be nervous. Despite your feelings, you should start preparing for the college application process. It starts now! Follow these tips to make sure that you are able to successful during this crucial part of your academic career. At this point, you may or may not have taken your SAT. If you are on the latter part, then you should be preparing to take your SAT. If you already took the test then you should give yourself a pat on the back because you just knocked out an important part of the college application process. Although you have gotten a lot done, you should be preparing to take the SAT again to attempt to get the highest score you can achieve. The next thing you should do is start asking your teachers for recommendations. Beat the crowd! Recommendations are necessary for college applications and scholarships. In addition, this is a great time to start visiting colleges. Make a list of the schools you would like to visit over the summer because before you know it the time to apply will come. Another good idea, start planning for senior year and what you hope to accomplish. Well that’s all the advice that I have for the spring of your junior year.

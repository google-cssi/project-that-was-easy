Fall of Your Senior Year!

It's the beginning of your college application race! Common App has opened. Colleges are eagerly awaiting your applications. During your first weeks of your senior year, you should meet with your high school counselor and or college advisor to discuss your college plans. Request letters of recommendation from your teachers, make sure to provide them with a resume and a date when you need it. Don't leave letters of recommendation for the last minute, you want to give your teachers enough time to write a superb letter. Don't forget to:

Continue applying to scholarships. Complete your standardized tests, and submit official scores to your colleges. Reach out to colleges for any questions.
